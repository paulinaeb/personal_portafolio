About

Hey! I'm Paulina Espejo, I'm Venezuelan and currently I'm a Computer Science Engineering 4th year student at Andrés Bello Catholic University. Since I learned programming, I've liked to join back-end and database development team, but I've also worked as front-end developer and I focus on the accessibility we can give to web pages from this branch.


About the programming languages and frameworks I've worked with, I use JavaScript, HTML, CSS, SQL, React, PHP and Java. I've been growing professionally on each one of them thanks to University projects, freelance and Platzi Courses. I've enjoyed every knowledge process. Mi dream is to work on a place where I can offer efficient solutions to enterprises, organizations and any person no matter their origin. What motivates me the most is that I want to influence and help more persons around the world through my work and something that I enjoy a lot to do: programming. As a hobby, I use to design digital content for publicity or promote any product or service, for me or for persons near me.


Spanish is my native language, but since I was a child, I've liked English and I used to learn everything I could by myself. Along time, I took basic and advanced English courses, so I improved my English skills and now I can read, write and speak fluently English and Spanish, as I always wanted to. There's an option "Spanish" and there you can find a spanish version of this portfolio. This is a small space where you can get to know about my experience as a programmer and my knowledge, so I invite you to browse this website and you can also contact me to talk about technology, accessibility, databases or to get any project done.
